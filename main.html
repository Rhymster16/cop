About Techfest

Techfest, IIT Bombay is Asia's Largest Science and Technology Festival. It serves as a platform to connect students, industry professionals, and international organizations, hosting a wide array of competitions, exhibitions, lectures, and workshops. With participants from over 2500 colleges across India and 500+ international students, it is a hub of innovation and inspiration.

Events

Techfest features a broad range of events including robotics competitions, coding challenges, hackathons, hardware showcases, quizzes, and technical exhibitions. From high schoolers to professionals, there's something for everyone to learn and explore.

Speakers

Every year, Techfest hosts globally renowned speakers from fields like technology, space, science, business, and policy. Past speakers include Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, Rakesh Sharma, and Nobel Laureates who have shared their journeys and insights with an enthusiastic audience.
